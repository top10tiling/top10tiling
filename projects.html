Our Quality Focus

What We Look At On Every Project

Preparation

We review the site condition, surface preparation, tile type, measurements, access, power, water and readiness before starting works.

Layout & Alignment

We consider tile layout, spacing, grout lines, edges, corners, trims, drains and visible finish areas before installation.

Installation

We focus on accurate tile placement, levelling, cutting, handling, adhesive coverage and detailed finishing.

Finishing

We complete grout, silicone, trims, edges and final clean-up of the tiling work area.

Project Information

Important Notes About Project Images

Project images shown on this website are provided as examples of tiling style, finishes and service categories. Actual results may vary depending on the tile type, materials, site conditions, surface preparation, project scope and client selections.

For an accurate quote, Top 10 Tiling may request photos, measurements, project address, tile details and site access information.

Before commencing works, clients should confirm tile type, tile size, layout, finish requirements, waterproofing requirements, access and payment terms in writing.

Standards & Compliance

Professional Workmanship & Records

Top 10 Tiling aims to complete works in line with applicable Australian construction standards and NSW requirements where relevant.

Relevant legislation and standards may include the Home Building Act 1989 (NSW), Australian Consumer Law, Work Health and Safety Act 2011 (NSW), Building and Construction Industry Security of Payment Act 1999 (NSW), National Construction Code, AS 3958.1 for ceramic tile installation and AS 3740 for waterproofing domestic wet areas.

We recommend written quotes, written variations, invoices, payment records, site photos and communication records for all projects.
