Who We Are

Tiling done right the first time.

Top 10 Tiling is a Sydney based tiling business focused on premium workmanship, accurate installation, strong finishes and professional communication.

We provide residential and commercial tiling services including bathroom tiling, floor tiling, wall tiling, kitchen splashbacks, waterproofing, large format tiles, outdoor tiling and stone finishes.

Our goal is to deliver clean, modern and long-lasting tiling solutions for homeowners, builders, renovation projects and commercial spaces.

Top 10 Tiling aims to complete works in line with applicable Australian construction standards and NSW requirements where relevant.
