Important Notes Before Booking

Materials

If tiles or materials are supplied by the client, builder or third party, Top 10 Tiling is not responsible for defective, damaged, warped, delayed or unsuitable materials.

Variations

Any changes to tile size, design, pattern, scope or installation requirements may affect the price, labour and project timeframe. Variations should be confirmed in writing.

Site Access

Clients must provide safe access to the work area, electricity, water, clear working space and suitable site conditions before works commence.

Payments

Payments must be made according to the agreed quote or contract. Late payment may cause work delays, suspension of works or recovery action.

Standards & Compliance

Professional Workmanship & Industry Standards

We aim to complete works in line with applicable Australian construction standards and NSW requirements where relevant.

Relevant legislation and standards may include the Home Building Act 1989 (NSW), Australian Consumer Law, Work Health and Safety Act 2011 (NSW), Building and Construction Industry Security of Payment Act 1999 (NSW), National Construction Code, AS 3958.1 for ceramic tile installation and AS 3740 for waterproofing domestic wet areas.
