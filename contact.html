Before Contacting Us

Information That Helps Your Quote

Providing clear project information helps us prepare a more accurate quote and project estimate.

Project Details

  • Bathroom, kitchen or outdoor area
  • Tile size and tile type
  • Approximate measurements
  • Photos of the work area
  • Existing floor or wall condition
  • Preferred project timeframe

Site Requirements

  • Access to electricity and water
  • Safe and clear work areas
  • Materials available on site
  • Parking access where possible
  • Written approval for changes
  • Deposit or agreed stage payment

Important Notes

  • Quotes are based on agreed scope of works
  • Changes may affect price and timing
  • Client supplied materials remain client responsibility
  • Site conditions may impact installation requirements
  • Written communication is recommended for variations
  • Completion dates are estimates only

Professional Records

  • Written quotes and invoices
  • Project photos and records
  • Variation approvals
  • Payment records
  • Communication records
  • Completion documentation

Legal & Compliance

Professional Standards

Top 10 Tiling aims to complete works in line with applicable Australian standards and NSW construction requirements where relevant.

Relevant legislation and standards may include the Home Building Act 1989 (NSW), Australian Consumer Law, Work Health and Safety Act 2011 (NSW), Building and Construction Industry Security of Payment Act 1999 (NSW), National Construction Code, AS 3958.1 for ceramic tile installation and AS 3740 for waterproofing domestic wet areas.

This website provides general information only and does not replace a written quote, written contract, site inspection or legal advice.
