We may collect personal and project information when you contact us, request a quote, submit a form, send photos, call, email, message us or engage our services.

Top 10 Tiling may use your information for business and project management purposes, including:

  • Preparing quotes and estimates

  • Managing tiling projects
  • Issuing invoices and payment records

  • Managing variations and changes
  • Keeping project records and evidence
  • Improving our services and website
  • Complying with legal, insurance and accounting requirements

When you submit a quote request or contact form, the information you provide may be sent to Top 10 Tiling by email or stored for customer service and project follow-up.

Please do not submit sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for your enquiry.

5. Project Photos, Videos & Site Information

Project photos and videos may be used to assess site conditions, prepare quotes, document progress, record defects, confirm completion or support project records.

Top 10 Tiling may use completed project photos for portfolio, website, social media or marketing purposes unless the client requests otherwise in writing.

We may keep payment records, invoice numbers, project costs and transaction references for accounting, tax, debt recovery and business record purposes.

Users may manage Google advertising settings through their Google account or browser settings.

Top 10 Tiling does not control third-party advertising cookies or external websites linked from advertisements.

9. Google Analytics & Website Tracking

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website, including pages viewed, time spent on the website, general location, device type and traffic sources.

This information is used to improve website performance, customer experience and marketing.

10. Disclosure of Information

Top 10 Tiling does not sell personal information.

We may disclose information where reasonably necessary to:
